Facealignment

It uses a two-level boosted regression with the shape indexed features. It's an implementation of the paper Face Alignment by Explicit Shape Regression

Dataset

Our model is trained by the Helen dataset.

Result

Our implementation could get an accurate result in around 300 ms on the Android phone with a Snapdragon 801 CPU.
